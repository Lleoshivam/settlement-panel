FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about loan settlement and our process.

Is this legal and safe?
Yes, absolutely. Loan settlement is a legally recognized process in India. We work within RBI guidelines and ensure all negotiations and agreements are legally documented and binding.
Will my credit score be affected?
A settled loan may temporarily impact your CIBIL score. However, we guide you on how to rebuild your credit after settlement. For most clients, relief from debt far outweighs the short-term score impact.
How much do your services cost?
We work on a commission-based model — we only earn when we successfully help you settle. The initial consultation is completely free with no obligations.
Which banks and lenders do you work with?
We work with all major public and private sector banks, NBFCs, and digital lenders including SBI, HDFC, ICICI, Axis, Bajaj Finance, IDFC, and many more.
How long does the settlement process take?
Settlement timelines vary by case, but most cases resolve within 30–90 days. We keep you informed at every stage and work as quickly as possible.
